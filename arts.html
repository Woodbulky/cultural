Classical Music

Major traditions of Indian classical music.

Hindustani Music

North Indian classical music featuring ragas, developed under Persian and Mughal influence. Instruments include sitar, tabla, and sarangi.

Carnatic Music

South Indian classical tradition with emphasis on vocal performance and composition. Instruments include veena, violin, and mridangam.

Traditional Paintings

Exquisite painting styles from different regions.

Madhubani

Vibrant folk paintings from Bihar using natural dyes and geometric patterns.

Warli Art

Tribal art from Maharashtra traditionally created on mud walls.

Tanjore

South Indian paintings known for gold foil work and semi-precious stones.

Textiles

A legacy of weaving, embroidery, and fabric art.

Weaving Traditions

India's textile heritage is legendary. From the fine muslins of Bengal to the vibrant silks of Kanchipuram, each region boasts unique weaving techniques.

  • Banarasi Silk (Varanasi)
  • Pashmina Wool (Kashmir)
  • Chanderi (Madhya Pradesh)
  • Kanjeevaram (Tamil Nadu)
