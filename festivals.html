Major National Festivals

Celebrations that unite people across India.

October/November

Diwali

The Festival of Lights celebrates victory of good over evil with lamps, rangolis, and fireworks.

March

Holi

The Festival of Colors marks spring's arrival with vibrant celebrations.

September/October

Durga Puja

Bengal's grandest festival honors Goddess Durga with elaborate pandals.

September/October

Navratri

Nine nights of devotion with Garba dances culminating in Dussehra.

August/September

Ganesh Chaturthi

Lord Ganesha's birthday with grand processions and visarjan ceremony.

September/October

Dussehra

Vijayadashami marks the victory of good over evil, celebrated with Ravana Dahan and processions.

December

Christmas

Celebrated with joy and enthusiasm across India, especially in Goa, Kerala, and the Northeast.

Regional Celebrations

Unique festivals showcasing India's cultural diversity.

Kerala

Onam

Kerala's harvest festival with Pookalam flower carpets and Onam Sadya feast.

Tamil Nadu

Pongal

Tamil Nadu's four-day harvest festival thanking the Sun God.
